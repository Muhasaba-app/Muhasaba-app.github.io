Muhasaba App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-01-25

This Privacy Policy applies to the Muhasaba App (hereby referred to as the “Application”) for mobile devices, created as a free service. The Application is provided “AS IS”.

1. Data Collection

The Application does not collect or transmit any personal data to the Service Provider or to any external servers.

The Application stores user-entered data (such as habit completion records) locally on the user’s device only using Apple’s on-device storage technologies. This data never leaves the device and is not accessible to the Service Provider.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate with or interact with any third-party services, analytics tools, or external APIs.

3. Advertising

The Application is ad-free and does not use any advertising networks.

4. Cookies and Tracking

The Application does not use cookies, trackers, analytics, or similar technologies.

5. Data Access and Control

All data created within the Application remains under the user’s control and is stored solely on their device. Deleting the Application will remove all locally stored data.

5. Updates to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
